Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to use RoastMyDorm?

RoastMyDorm is completely free for students! We don't charge any platform fees. You only pay the rent directly to the landlord.

Are all properties verified?

Yes! All our listings go through a verification process to ensure they meet our quality standards and are legitimate rental opportunities.

How do I contact a landlord?

You can contact landlords through our integrated chat system, make direct phone calls, or use WhatsApp. All contact methods are available on each property listing.

Can I schedule a viewing?

Absolutely! Once you contact the landlord, you can arrange a convenient time to visit the property. Most landlords are flexible with scheduling.

What cities do you cover?

We currently cover Casablanca, Rabat, and Marrakech, with plans to expand to more Moroccan cities soon.

Is payment secure?

Yes! We encourage face-to-face payments for maximum security. This ensures both parties are protected and can complete the transaction safely.
