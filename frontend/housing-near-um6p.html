Getting to UM6P Campus

  • UM6P Free Shuttle — Daily bus from Marrakech (Bab Doukkala / Gare Routière) to Benguerir campus. Free for enrolled students. Check the current semester timetable with UM6P student services.
  • ONCF Train — Marrakech–Benguerir rail line runs several times per day. Train ticket costs around 25–40 MAD. Station is a 10-min taxi from campus.
  • Grand taxis (Benguerir) — From Benguerir town centre to campus: 10–20 MAD shared, 40–60 MAD private.
  • Bicycle (Benguerir town) — The town is small and flat. Students living in Benguerir itself can cycle to campus in under 15 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does UM6P provide on-campus housing for all students?
UM6P has a large on-campus residence with furnished rooms at 500–1,500 MAD/month including utilities. Capacity is generous but not unlimited. Apply during the admissions process — your offer letter will typically include housing allocation details. Contact UM6P student services if you have questions.
Can I live in Marrakech and commute to UM6P daily?
Yes — UM6P runs a free shuttle bus between Marrakech and Benguerir campus. The journey takes about 45–50 minutes. Many students live in Gueliz or Daoudiate and use this shuttle. It works well as long as your schedule allows for the commute time each morning and evening.
How much does it cost to live in Benguerir town?
Benguerir is a small, affordable city. Studios run 800–2,000 MAD/month. Shared apartments are 800–1,500 MAD per person. It's significantly cheaper than Marrakech. The trade-off is fewer entertainment options — most students go to Marrakech on weekends.
Is there a train between Benguerir and Marrakech?
Yes — ONCF operates train service between Marrakech and Benguerir. The journey takes about 35–45 minutes and costs 25–40 MAD. It's a good alternative to the shuttle for students with flexible schedules, though the UM6P shuttle is free.
What facilities does UM6P on-campus housing include?
UM6P's on-campus residences are known for their quality — furnished rooms with WiFi, access to sports facilities (swimming pool, tennis, football), cafeteria, and 24-hour security. All utilities are typically included in the monthly fee of 500–1,500 MAD.
