Best Neighborhoods for UPM Students

UPM campus sits on Route de Casablanca, making it accessible from Gueliz (trendy, central), Saada and Izdihar (closer, more affordable), and Targa and Massira (further but very budget-friendly). Most students with higher budgets choose Gueliz; those looking to save money choose Saada or Izdihar and walk or cycle to campus.

Getting to UPM Campus

  • Petit taxis — The most convenient option. 15–25 MAD from Gueliz, 10–18 MAD from Saada or Izdihar. Taxis are everywhere in Marrakech.
  • Bicycle / Scooter — Students in Saada and Izdihar are close enough to cycle in 10–15 minutes. Very popular and saves money on daily commuting.
  • City Bus line 16 — Connects Gueliz to Route de Casablanca. 3 MAD per ride. Slower but very cheap for daily commuters.
  • Tramway L1 — Serves Gueliz and Daoudiate, with a stop closer to Route de Casa. Useful if you live on the tram corridor.
  • InDrive / Careem — Available in Marrakech. Budget 20–40 MAD for a ride from Gueliz to campus.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where exactly is UPM's campus in Marrakech?
UPM is on Route de Casablanca, northwest of the Marrakech city center. The campus is accessible from Gueliz in about 10–15 minutes by taxi, or 15–20 minutes by bicycle from Saada and Izdihar.
What's the cheapest area to live near UPM?
Targa and Massira offer the lowest rents — shared rooms from 1,500 MAD. Saada and Izdihar are slightly more expensive but significantly closer to campus, making them better value overall when you factor in daily taxi savings.
Is living in Gueliz worth the extra cost for UPM students?
It depends on your priorities. Gueliz has Marrakech's best cafés, sports facilities, supermarkets, and social life — great for well-being during a demanding degree. If you share a 3-bed apartment with two friends, the per-person cost drops to 2,000–2,500 MAD, which becomes much more manageable.
Can I find furnished rooms near UPM?
Yes — furnished studios and rooms in shared apartments are common near Route de Casa. Expect to pay 300–500 MAD/month more than unfurnished equivalents. Always check that a bed, wardrobe, and kitchen basics are included before signing.
Does UPM have an on-campus residence?
UPM has a small on-campus residence with limited availability. Contact the Student Services office when you register. Most students find private housing in Gueliz, Saada, or the surrounding areas.
