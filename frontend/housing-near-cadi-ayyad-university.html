Getting Around Marrakech

  • Tramway L1 — Connects Gueliz, Semlalia, Daoudiate, Massira, and Route de Casa. Covers most UCA faculties. 4 MAD flat fare.
  • Petit taxis — Metered, very affordable. 10–25 MAD between any student neighbourhoods and campus. Widely available.
  • Bicycle — Marrakech is flat. Students in Semlalia, Daoudiate, and Massira cycle to campus in 10–20 minutes.
  • City buses — Lines 1, 11, 16 serve most student areas. 3 MAD per ride.
  • Careem / InDrive — Available in Marrakech for door-to-door trips in 5 minutes. 15–35 MAD from most areas to campus.

Student Housing Tips for Marrakech

Marrakech rental listings disappear fast at the start of each academic year (September). Visit in July–August if possible, or ask a friend already in the city to check listings. Here are the key things to watch:

  • Verify the water heater — Some older buildings in Massira and Dyur Marjane have collective chauffage (building-wide heating). Check if hot water is available 24/7.
  • Ask about caution (deposit) — Standard is 1–2 months' rent. Get a receipt and a written lease.
  • Check internet options — Maroc Telecom and Inwi both offer fibre in Gueliz and Daoudiate. Massira coverage is more variable.
  • Summer subletting — Many UCA students sublet their rooms in June–July. This is a good way to save money while studying.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which neighborhood is closest to UCA Sciences Semlalia?
Semlalia district is walking distance to the faculty. Daoudiate and Massira are 10–15 minutes by tram or bicycle. These three neighbourhoods have the most student housing and offer prices from 1,200–3,000 MAD/month for shared rooms and studios.
Is Gueliz a good place to live as a UCA student?
Gueliz is excellent for lifestyle — good cafés, gyms, supermarkets, and nightlife. It's most convenient for FSJEP and ENCG students. For Sciences Semlalia, it's 20 minutes by tram. Studios in Gueliz run 2,500–5,000 MAD, which is higher, but colocation brings costs down to 1,800–2,500 MAD per person.
Does UCA have subsidised student residences?
Yes — CNOUS-managed residences are available near Semlalia campus and on Route de Casablanca. Monthly cost is 400–800 MAD, heavily subsidised. Capacity is very limited; apply immediately upon admission and meet with the Bureau des Affaires Estudiantines.
What's the average rent in Daoudiate for students?
Shared rooms in Daoudiate cost 1,400–2,000 MAD/month per person. A full 2-bedroom apartment runs 3,000–5,000 MAD, making it 1,500–2,500 MAD per person when shared. Daoudiate is well connected by tram to most UCA faculties.
How early should I look for housing near UCA?
Start looking in July for a September move-in. The best listings near UCA go quickly, especially around Semlalia and Daoudiate. If you're coming from another city, connect with UCA student Facebook groups or use RoastMyDorm to browse verified listings remotely.
