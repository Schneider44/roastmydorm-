Marrakech — ISPITS Menara

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are the ISPITS campuses located?
ISPITS operates campuses across Morocco. The main student-relevant campuses are in Casablanca (affiliated with CHU Ibn Rochd in Ain Sebaa) and Marrakech (affiliated with CHU Mohammed VI near the Menara). Check your admission letter for your exact campus address.
How much does rent cost near ISPITS?
In Casablanca, shared rooms near Ain Sebaa start at 1,500 MAD. In Marrakech, rooms in Massira can be found from 1,200 MAD. Most ISPITS students share apartments of 3–4 people to reduce costs significantly.
Do ISPITS students get priority for CNOUS residences?
Some ISPITS students qualify for subsidised CNOUS residences near the affiliated hospitals, particularly scholarship holders from rural areas. Ask at the campus administration office (Bureau des Affaires Estudiantines) when you register.
What's the best area to live in near ISPITS Marrakech?
Massira is the closest and cheapest neighbourhood — 10 minutes by tram or taxi to CHU Mohammed VI. Daoudiate is slightly further but very popular with students. Gueliz is more expensive but has the best cafés, supermarkets, and social life.
Can I find a furnished room near ISPITS?
Yes — furnished studios and furnished rooms in shared flats are common near both campuses. Expect to pay 200–400 MAD/month more than unfurnished equivalents. Always verify what "meublé" includes — a bed and wardrobe at minimum, ideally a full kitchen setup.
