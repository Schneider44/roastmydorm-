Best Neighborhoods for ENCG Students

ENCG Casablanca sits along Route d'El Jadida in the Ain Sebaa district, east of the city center. Students on a tight budget cluster in Ben M'sik and Oulfa; those wanting easier access to campus stay in Ain Sebaa itself; and those who prioritize lifestyle over commute opt for Maarif and take the T2 tram.

Getting to Campus

  • Tramway T2 — Runs from Lissasfa through Hay Hassani, Maarif, and Ain Sebaa. Stops at Ain Sebaa closest to ENCG campus. 7 MAD flat fare.
  • RATC Bus lines 3, 7, 10 — Cover Route d'El Jadida and Ben M'sik corridor. 4 MAD per ride.
  • Grand taxis — Direct routes from Ben M'sik and Derb Sultan to Ain Sebaa. 8–15 MAD shared, 40–60 MAD private.
  • Careem / InDrive — 25–50 MAD from Maarif. Faster than bus during peak hours.
  • On foot — Students in Ain Sebaa can walk to campus in 10–20 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is ENCG Casablanca located?
ENCG Casablanca is on Route d'El Jadida in the Ain Sebaa district. The campus is about 9 km from the city center, near the Route d'El Jadida tram stop on the T2 line.
What is the average rent near ENCG Casablanca?
Shared rooms in Ben M'sik or Ain Sebaa start at 1,800 MAD/month. A studio in Ain Sebaa runs 2,500–4,000 MAD. If you prefer Maarif for the lifestyle, expect 3,000–5,000 MAD for a studio plus 30–40 min tram commute.
How do students commute to ENCG?
Most students use Tramway T2, which stops near campus and runs frequently from 6 AM to 11 PM. Grand taxis from Ben M'sik are also cheap (8–12 MAD shared). Those living in Ain Sebaa itself can walk.
Is there on-campus housing at ENCG Casablanca?
ENCG has a limited CNOUS-managed residence hall on campus. Priority is given to scholarship students from outside Casablanca. Most students live off-campus in the surrounding neighbourhoods.
What should I look for when renting near ENCG?
Check proximity to a T2 tram stop, water heater (chauffe-eau), internet connection, and whether the building has an elevator (ascenseur). Avoid listings without a written lease — even a simple contrat de bail protects your deposit.
